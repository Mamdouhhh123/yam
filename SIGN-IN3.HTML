Our story:
YAM was founded by Abdulrahman Ashraf and Youssef Hossny in 2020 with a vision of providing life-transforming learning experiences to learners around the world. Today,YAM is a global online learning platform that offers anyone, anywhere access to online courses and degrees from leading universities and companies
YAM recieved B corp certfication in FEB 2021,which means that we have a legal duty not only to our shareholders , but to also make a positive impact on society more broadly, as we continue our efforts to reduce barriers to world-class education for all.
